AI Powered MDM & Entity Resolution

4DAlert enhances MDM with Match & Merge to connect multi-domain records, eliminate duplicates, and ensure accuracy. Its Survivorship logic prioritizes the most reliable source for master records. Pre-built Content for SAP & Salesforce ensures seamless integration across key platforms. AI-Powered MDM enables smarter, automated data management, improving data quality over time.

Automated Data Reconciliation

4DAlert automates the reconciliation of data between your analytics platform and source systems after every data load. By using AI/ML-powered algorithms, it ensures data accuracy and detects discrepancies before they impact decision-making. This proactive approach allows you to identify and resolve data issues early, preventing problems from reaching your users.

Data Quality & Observability

With 4DAlerts data observability module, you can continuously monitor the health of your data across all platforms. Using machine learning, it automatically detects anomalies, generates adaptive rules, and identifies the root causes of issues. This helps you prevent data quality problems before they arise, ensuring reliable, actionable data for your teams.

CI/CD Pipeline Automation

4DAlert automates CI/CD pipelines, Schema comparison, and Change deployment to maintain consistent, compliant, and error-free data environments. It seamlessly synchronizes database schemas, minimizing deployment errors and accelerating rollouts. With built-in compliance checks and version control, 4DAlert ensures regulatory adherence while maintaining a complete history of schema changes, reducing manual intervention and ensuring systems remain secure and up-to-date.

Schema Compare & Version Control

4DAlerts Schema Compare & Version Control ensures consistency across all database environments by detecting differences between objectstables, views, functions, procedures, and indexesand synchronizing them accurately. It streamlines deployments with automated change scripts, reduces manual errors, and maintains a clear history of schema updates, enabling teams to collaborate efficiently and keep development, testing, and production fully aligned.

Entity Relationship Modeling

Entity-Relationship (ER) Diagrams are essential tools in database design, providing a visual framework that illustrates data entities and their relationships. With 4DAlert, the process of creating and managing ER diagrams is streamlined, ensuring that businesses maintain databases that are consistent, accurate, and optimized.
